# Hyper Launcher Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 15, 2025

## Overview

Hyper Launcher is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Data Collection

### Information We Collect
- **Bookmark URLs and Titles**: Stored locally in your browser for bookmark management functionality
- **Extension Settings**: View preferences, layout settings, and tab behavior preferences
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics (no personal data) to improve the extension

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identifying information
- Browsing history outside of bookmarks you explicitly save
- Passwords or sensitive data
- Data from websites you visit

## Data Usage

### How We Use Your Data
- **Bookmark Management**: URLs and titles are used solely for displaying and organizing your bookmarks
- **Settings Persistence**: Preferences are stored to maintain your customized experience
- **Performance Improvement**: Anonymous analytics help us identify and fix issues

### No External Transmission
- All bookmark data is stored locally in your browser
- No personal data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All bookmark data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API
- Data remains on your device and is not uploaded to our servers
- You maintain full control over your data

### Data Retention
- Data is retained until you manually delete bookmarks or uninstall the extension
- Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data

## Third-Party Services

### Favicon Loading
- The extension may load website favicons (small icons) from the original websites
- This is done using standard web requests and does not share your personal data
- No tracking or analytics data is collected through favicon requests

### No Third-Party Data Sharing
- We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties
- No advertising or tracking networks are integrated into the extension

## User Rights

### Your Control Over Data
- **Export**: You can export all your bookmark data at any time
- **Delete**: You can delete individual bookmarks or all data
- **Transparency**: All data is stored locally and accessible to you

### Data Portability
- The extension provides export functionality to download your bookmarks
- Exported data is in standard JSON format for easy migration

## Security

### Data Protection
- All data is stored using Chrome's secure storage APIs
- The extension follows Chrome Web Store security policies
- Input validation prevents malicious data injection

### No Remote Access
- We cannot access your bookmark data remotely
- No backend servers store or process your personal information

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed toward children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated effective date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **Storage**: To save your bookmarks and settings locally
- **Active Tab**: To get the current page information when adding bookmarks
- **Context Menus**: To add right-click menu options for adding bookmarks
- **Notifications**: To show confirmation messages when bookmarks are added

Optional permissions (requested only when needed):
- **Tabs**: To open bookmarks in new tabs
- **Downloads**: To export your bookmark data
- **Bookmarks**: To add Hyper Launcher to your browser's bookmark bar
- **Alarms**: For periodic maintenance tasks

## Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the extension's privacy practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.

## Compliance

This extension complies with:
- Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements

---

*This privacy policy applies only to the Hyper Launcher Chrome extension and does not cover any websites you may visit using bookmarks stored in the extension.*
