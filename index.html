States & Classes 
Icon Types:
‚Ä¢ Default Icon: Emoji in .app-icon element
‚Ä¢ Favicon: .app-icon.has-favicon with background-image

‚Ä¢ Default: Base .app-tile styling

‚Ä¢ Dragging: .app-tile.dragging (semi-transparent, rotated)
